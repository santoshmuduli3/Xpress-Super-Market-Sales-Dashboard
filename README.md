# Xpress-Super-Market-Sales-Dashboard

**Key Business Insights**
1. Clothing is the best-selling category, contributing over 62% of total quantity sold.
2. Printers are the most profitable product category.
3. Maharashtra is the highest revenue-generating state.
4. November is the most profitable month, while May records the biggest loss.
5. Cash on Delivery remains the preferred payment method.
6. A few loyal customers contribute significantly to overall sales.

**Business Recommendations**
1. Focus marketing and inventory on the Clothing category to capitalize on its strong demand.
2. Increase promotions for Printers and Bookcases, as they generate the highest profits.
3. Investigate the causes of losses in May, July, September, and December, such as excessive discounts, returns, or higher operating costs.
4. Strengthen customer loyalty programs for top-spending customers like Harivansh and Madhav.
5. Expand sales efforts in Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh, while developing strategies to improve performance in lower-selling states.
6. Encourage digital payment adoption (UPI and cards) through cashback offers to reduce dependence on Cash on Delivery.
